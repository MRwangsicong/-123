How $Shika The Legend.

According to Shinto(East asia religion) mythology, Takemikazuchi, a prominent deity of thunder and martial prowess, traveled from Kashima Shrine in present-day Ibaraki Prefecture to Nara riding a white deer. This event signified the divine approval and protection over the region, establishing the deer as sacred creatures. Kasuga Taisha Shrine, established in 768 CE, honors this deity and, by extension, the deer that are considered his messengers.
